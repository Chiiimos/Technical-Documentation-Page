UNBOXING & ORGANIZING

  • Open Box
  • Remove all Runners, The Manual, and Stickers
  • Lay everything on a table
  • Check Runners labels to the match the manual
  • Keep runners in order
UNBOXING & ORGANIZING
READING THE MANUAL

The manual contains:

  • Runner map
  • Part numbers
  • Assembly steps
  • Sticker placement

Tip: Follow the manual exactly in order. Gunpla is designed to snap-fit; no glue needed.

MANUAL
CUTTING PARTS FROM RUNNERS

  • Find the part you need
  • Cut slightly away from the nub, leaving a small stub
  • Cut the remaining nub with the nipper or hobby knife
  • Sand the nub area lightly with 800–1200 grit until smooth

Safety tip: Always cut away from your body

ASSEMBLING PARTS

Gunpla uses snap-fit engineering, so parts click together

Basic Assembly Order

  • Head Unit
  • Torso/Chest Unit
  • Arms
  • LEGS
  • WAIST UNIT
  • WEAPONS
  • FINAL ASSEMBLY

Tips

  • Press firmly but gently.
  • Check orientation so parts don’t snap incorrectly.
  • Don’t force pieces; if it won’t fit, re-check the manual.
